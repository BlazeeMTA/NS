Graphics

Supposedly a new and improved version of PUBG Mobile,
PUBG New State takes players into a futuristic world devastated by battles but also still pretty cool-looking.
The map of Troi itself is sleek and technologically advanced, despite the battle-damaged look.
In particular, I appreciated the tram that travels across the map on its own,
offering players a bit of a safe haven when they need to take a breather (or a quick ride).

Top 15 games on mobile

PUBG New State offers a fun battle royale experience for anyone who's looking for a so-called "new game" on mobile.
It's great to hop into a match or two every so often, but it's not something I'll purposely lose sleep over any time soon,
as there are sadly better ways to spend my screen time on mobile out there.
But if you're hungry for a fresh take on the franchise, at the very least, the game should be enough to scratch that itch.
