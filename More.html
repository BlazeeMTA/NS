NEW STATE Labs Opens for Experiments

Master the Art of Stealth on the Battleground

The collaboration with Ubisoft’s best-selling series, Assassin’s Creed,
is live in NEW STATE MOBILE and runs until Sept. 22. During the collaboration period,
players can get the full outfits of two iconic characters from the hit series: Ezio Auditore da Firenze,
beloved character of the Assassin’s Creed franchise since Assassin’s Creed II,
and Shay Patrick Cormac from Assassin’s Creed: Rogue. Players can also obtain over 30 unique Assassin’s Creed-themed costumes,
weapon skins and items through limited-time events and collaboration crates.

NEW STATE Labs Opens for Experiments

NEW STATE Labs is now open and is designed to provide experimental content for players to experience new settings
and features in the game before everyone else.
The Lab’s first test feature is Akinta: Bounty Royale,
a project that involves competing for a prize instead of pure survival skills.
It will be available for access through to September 22.
A maximum of 36 players can join the match in third-person squad mode on the new upcoming map, Akinta.
A round of play tickets has been given to a selection of players and others can also take part in the project by receiving an invitation ticket.
Such invitation tickets can be obtained from the selected participants or earned through community events.
